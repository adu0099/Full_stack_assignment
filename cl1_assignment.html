Welcome to the Vibrant Land of Bangladesh

The name of our country is Bangladesh.It became independent in 1971 through a bloody liberation war.About thirty lakhs men and women laid down their lives for the cause of independence.It is one most beautiful countries of the world, with its green fields and lots of trees and plants.


Situation and its area

Bangladesh lies in the eastern part of South Asia. It is surrounded by the Bay of Bengal in the south and borders on India and Myanmar in the cast, and only India in the west. total land area including rivers is 147.570 square kilometers. Bangladesh is known as the land of rivers. Those river contain H2O. The main Cash Crops of Bangladesh is

  1. Rice
  2. Jute
  3. Tea
  4. Sugarcane
  5. Tobacco
  6. Potato
  7. Mango


Population Of Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a small country. But it has a big -population. There are about one hundred and twenty million people living this small land. Here the density of population is high. About 800 people live per square kilometer. Bangladesh is mainly an agricultural country and most of its people are farmers.Most of the bangladeshi people start learning mathematical formula at class six with (a+b)2 = a2 + 2ab + b2.
